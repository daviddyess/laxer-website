About US

Laxer OS is an Arch Linux-based distribution of Linux from PAKISTAN for work and everyday use. Our OS is available for any 64-bit computer, laptop, or tablet. Our distribution focuses on security, reliability, and performance, You will notice the significant performance boost on the first boot of your system, we are determined to provide you a platform to Get Things Done.

Laxer OS is a versatile, and simple Linux distribution designed to fit the needs of the competent and common Linux users. It is powerful, easy to manage and install, making it an ideal distribution for computer laptops workstations and tablets.

We ship it with a Gnome desktop environment that is efficient, stable, easy to use, and very customizable. We hope you enjoy our Linux distribution as much as we enjoy making it for you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
