About

Laxer OS is an Arch Linux-based distribution of Linux for work and everyday use, it is available for any 64-bit computer, laptop, or tablet with highly focused on security, reliability, and performance, Laxer OS is a versatile, and simple Linux distribution designed to fit the needs of the competent and common Linux users. It is powerful, easy to manage and install which makes it an ideal distribution for computer laptops workstations and tablets.
